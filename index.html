If you have an Agility (Personal Contract Plan) agreement and want to own your vehicle outright before your agreement is due to end, we can give you a settlement quote. It'll only be valid for a short time, so make sure you check the expiry date. If you have one of our other finance products, please get in touch with your Retailer who will be able to give you more information.

Your payments are based on the agreed annual permitted mileage in your finance agreement; if you go over this amount, there is an excess mileage fee which you’ll need to pay at the end of your agreement. There are more details about this on page 2 of your finance agreement.

Call us on 0370 847 0700 to talk through any issues you’re having. You’ll need your agreement number and vehicle registration number to hand. We’ll also ask you to confirm your address for data protection.
